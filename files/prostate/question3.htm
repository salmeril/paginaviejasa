Question 3: Are There Changes in Patients' Overall Physical Functioning and Mental Health After the Acupuncture Treatment?

     As Table 3 displays, most of the patients increased their overall physical functioning and mental health after the treatment. Figure 3 and Figure 4 display the same results in bar charts.
